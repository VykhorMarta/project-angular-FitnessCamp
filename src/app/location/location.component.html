Croatia -

is the place where the Mediterranean, the Alps and Pannonia meet. There are few countries in Europe, and in the whole world, where unique natural complexes and historical monuments, wonderful sea coast with hundreds of islands and numerous castles, dense forests and a lot of thermal springs coexist so miraculously.
